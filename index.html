About Us

What are Generational Hotels?

What is Gym Generator?

  • Residents when using the gym equipment generate electricity using a unique generator which produces almost double in one hour than one barrel can produce. The electricity is then sold toa utility companies that have net metering laws to guarantee each kWh is bought.
  • To sum up anyone who uses the hotel gym can generate energy which would lead the hotel receiving money.
  • The hotel then has many options as to what to do with this extra generated income (which is potentially enormous).

The Generator - How does it work

The generator works by combing the two main types of generators, standarad (w/brushes) and brushless. The two types of generators will be combined and rotated in yhe opposite direction of each other to massively increase efficiency. The thing that makes this generator special is the way it rotates but also the miltiple layers. It`s like a generator inside of another generator.

Finances For Generator

  • The amount someone can earn in an hr can vary. In tx each kWH is sold at 12¢ each. We are estimating to produce +3000 kWh per hr on a stationary bike if the rider maintains a speed of 12-16 mph. This estimate is based on Manoj Bhargava' s study with his generator. So, in TX, the stationary bike can make upwards of $360 per hr (between 12-16 mph)
  • The product if we can secure fabrication / manufacturing network, the price will drop but as of right now it is estimated to be roughly $18k.
  • This generator can be installed in all cardio equipment, elliptical, stationary bike, row machine, arm bike and treadmill.
